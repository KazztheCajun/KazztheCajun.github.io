About Me

Hello, welcome to my portfolio!

I'm an artist, freelance developer, and I like to make games/puzzles. To that end, I started Cajun Calamiy productions about a year ago in the hopes of creating unique and accessable games/apps/software for everyone. So far, our only release to date is the super handy GM Helper app, a system agnostic tool that helps track the HP and initiative of monsters and players, but we hope to have some new projects ready for the public soon.

We are also currently developing an app that helps track the health of your pet and also consolidate thier health info in one convienient place. It is in Beta at the moment and will be moving to Early Access edition within the year!

Apart my programming projects, I also like to create useable art, like ceramic bowls and cups, as well as more old school paintings. I draw a lot of inspiration from science and technology for use in my art. One of my dream projects is to recreate the periodic table of elements as a series of mixed media "paintings".
